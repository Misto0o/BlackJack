How to Play Blackjack

- Try to get cards totaling as close to 21 without going over.
- Aces count as 1 or 11, Game dynamically chooses for you
- Face cards are worth 10.
- Beat the dealer to win credits.
- In multiplayer mode, play against a friend.
- Place bets before each round.
